How we choose our picks

We don’t run our own timed smoke tests or lab measurements. Instead, we rely on a systematic analysis of long-term owner reviews (spanning 6 months to several years of use), published expert reviews from reputable outdoor-cooking sites, and patterns in common failures reported across forums. Things that actually break, door hinges, thermostat drift, rusted heating elements, tell us more than a single first-use impression. Every pick on this list has earned a strong reputation for consistent heat, solid build, and real-world longevity. We prioritize models that owners still recommend after their honeymoon period, and we note the trade-offs (like limited max temperature or smaller chip capacity) so you know exactly what you’re getting.
